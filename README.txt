things that boost influence temporarily:
- having other people nearby who are similarly influenced
- having certain influence-boosting items
- occupying a special position, like being on a stage
- having recently influenced other people

types of items:
- money
- food (increases energy)
- microphone (increases zone of influence)
- amulet (increases strength of influence)
- book (increases skill)
- other things that increase influence only for certain types of people

types of environments
- short grass (equal for everyone)
- tall grass (better for adventurer)
- indoors (better for marketer, entrepreneur
- concrete (better for street)
- auditorium (better for artist, politician)
- snow (worse for everyone)
- store (can buy/sell things)
- water (can't enter)
